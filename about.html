Hey, this section on my website is dedicated to telling you, my viewer something about myself. I am about halfway to really knowing myself. It's a process like getting to know another person, learning to be in tune with your honest thoughts and motives. Sometimes it is hard to introduce yourself, you would expect after 20 years i would know myself well enough to know where to start, well , i'll try to see what type of image i can portray to you of me.

I am a person who is neutral about every aspect of life. I believe our traits whether positive or negative makes us the person we are portrayed to the world.
I like to keep a smile on my face no matter what I am going through, and that smile keeps me going. As a person I believe life is an art and I am the artist of my life, I try to keep this art colourful and as positive and cheerful as possible.
There are many things I enjoy doing, Seeing and experiencing. For instance i enjoy music,
music in my opinion is the best stress reliever, there is nothing a good song and a clear mind cannot fix. I also enjoy a good movie, I tend to live my life
vicariously through these motion pictures.

Moreover, there are many other things I dislike but I tend to not know them off the top of my head until it appears in sight. Sometimes I get irritated by the slightest things
Another time it's things that are worth the fuss. At the end of the day this is me and I dont see myself changing for anyone but me.
My favourite quote or thing to say is a lyric from the song Blessings by Big Sean ft. Drake, Kanye West, "Im here for a good time not a long time"
